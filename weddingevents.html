Information For Our Guests:

Come celebrate with us with dinner and a bon fire! Dinner will be served around 5:00 p.m., however we will be there earlier in the afternoon for anyone who wants to come for a beach day. Free parking is available in the parking lot at Rio Del Mar Boulevard and Aptos Beach Drive. Street parking is also available. Bring warm clothes as it can get cool at night.

Driving Directions:

From CA-1/State Hwy 1:Take the Rio Del Mar Boulevard exit off of CA-1/State Hwy 1. Head west on Rio Del Mar Boulevard toward Beach Pines Drive. Turn right toward Esplanade. Turn left on Esplanade and park in parking lot or on the surrounding streets.
