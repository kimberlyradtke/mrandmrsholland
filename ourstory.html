All About Kristin

I was born in Kansas and raised in Chino Hills, California before I moved to Kansas City, Missouri about five years ago. I am currently a student at the University of Missouri, Kanas City and am studying to be a scenic designer. I love spending time with friends and family, creating art and reading. I am so happy that I met Matthew on my adventure in Kansas City and can not wait to have him as my husband.

All About Matthew

I was born and raised in Richmond, Missouri and eventually moved into the city of Kansas City (Missouri side). I am currently a Cell Tower Technician in Carson, California. I enjoy spending time with friends and family, having barbeques, going to the shooting range and drinking beer. I am so excited to make Kristin my Mrs. and can not wait to start our life together.

How We Met

I decided to move to Kansas City from southern California five years ago. I ended up getting a job at Raglan Road, an Irish pub in the Power and Light District. It was there that I met Matthew, who was a bartender. At first we were friends and then our friendship developed into a relationship. We have had a blast exploring the city, learning about each other, and going on adventures. Matthew has been a great friend, boyfriend, and now fiancee and I know that he will be a great husband.

We're Engaged!

We were about to embark on a great vacation that we had planned to southern California and Hawaii to visit family. The first stop was southern California. Matt had planned this engagement for a long time and knew the perfect time to take me by surprise. My family planned a day trip to the Stone Brewing Company for beer tasting. While we were enjoying delicious beer and food Matt asked me to take a walk through the garden. As we were walking one of the servers delivered us a surprise dessert! When I looked at the plate I saw a beer tasting flight, the big engagement question written in chocolate and a gorgeous engagement ring! I couldn't stop smiling when he asked me to be his wife and immediately said YES!
